How to add events to the ward board

To add events to the ward board, you send an email to wardboard.events@gmail.com. In order to do so, you must be approved to do so. If you are not approved, please contact the administrator. To add an event, your email's subject must start with "Add" and the contents must use specify the field followed by a colon and the details (ex. "Title: Ward BBQ"). The fields you can specify are Title, Date, Time, Location, and Description. An example is shown below:

wardboard.events@gmail.com

Add event

Title: Personal Finance Class

Date: 12/05/25

Time: 9:00 PM

Location: Church Building

Description: We will be working on our finances.

Omitting a field will result in default values being applied (either left blank or will indicate TBD). The title cannot be ommitted and is required.

To update a pre-existing event, simply ensure the Title is the same. Events with past dates will be deleted at midnight everyday.

How to remove events from the ward board

To remove events to the ward board, you send an email to wardboard.events@gmail.com. You must be approved to do this. To remove an event, your email's subject must start with "Remove" and the contents must use the following format:

wardboard.events@gmail.com

Remove event

Title: Personal Finance Class

Note: The title must match the pre-existing event's title you wish to delete.
